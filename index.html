TKIP Internship program summer 2025

Harrison Siegel: KYTC Department of Highways

Wrong Way Driving Project

The wrong way driving project is an example of a project I got to work on. The goal of this project is to prevent wrong way drivers on highway ramps. Methods used include detection cameras, signage, and illumanated warnings. I had the oppertunity to examine ramp and sign design, attend planning meetings, and visit sites included in the project. The images posted are examples of a wrong way driving prevention sign and an example ramp design on New Circle Road in Lexington.

SASHTO Confrence

Another part of my internship was working on the transportation schedules of the upcoming Southeastern Association of State Highway and Transportation Officials (SASHTO) Confrence being held in Lexington this August. For the confrence I had to help prepare a detailed schedule of all the transporation from hotels, events, board meetings, and more.
